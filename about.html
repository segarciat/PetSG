About PetSG

Hi, I am Sergio Garcia. PetSG is a fictional site that I made in order to practice my HTML and CSS skills. I decided on the pet theme because I recently adopted a cat, whose name is Peggy Sue. I learned about Peggy through a friend who worked at CUNY Hunter, after she had seen a posting about it. I went all the way to Long Island to get Peggy. Peggy's owner at the time could not keep Peggy because his wife was allergic, and prior to that she belonged to a lady who had unfortunately passed. I have now had Peggy for a couple of months, and I woudl say we have gotten close. I love her quite a bit.
