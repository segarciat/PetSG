About PetSG

DISCLAIMER: This page is a fictional, and none of the pets here are actually up for adoption. These pictures were taken from pexels.com. I do not claim ownership of any of the images in this website, except the ones in this about page.


Hi, I am Sergio Garcia. PetSG is a fictional site that I made in order to practice my HTML and CSS skills. I decided on the pet theme because I recently adopted a cat, whose name is Peggy Sue. I learned about Peggy through a friend who had seen a posting about her.


I went all the way to Long Island to get Peggy. Peggy's owner at the time could not keep Peggy because his wife was allergic, and prior to that she belonged to a lady who had unfortunately passed.


I have now had Peggy for a couple of months, and I love her. I think she's starting to feel the same way, too. Adopt a pet, and see how your life will change, along with that of your pet!


Please feel free to provide any feedback on how to make the website look cooler. If you would like to work on something together, feel free to submit a form to contact me, too!
