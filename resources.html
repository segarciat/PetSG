Veterinarian services

Your pet health comes first. Consider investing in a wellness plan for your pet! Potential benefits include:


  1. Required vaccinations
  2. Dental care
  3. Ability to visit a vet whenever you need
  4. Grooming services

There are a variety of suppliers for wellness plans, among them: Embrace, PetsBest, Banfield and PetSmart. Yet another option is health insurance, but I'll allow you to do the research on that

Pet Supplies

The little things make a big difference. Your pet is a family member, and family members get things for christmas right? That being said, don't wait until christmas to get your pet something to make her life more enjoyable. Some possibilities are:


  • Toys
  • Treats
  • Toiletries
  • Personal home

These will vary from pet to pet. For example, a reptile might also require a tank enclosure and appropriate light fixtures. A cat will thrive with a litter box, and some dogs might use a paper pads for their business. Treats are useful to reward your pet for behaving well and also for training them. If you have a little extra money and space, a cat tree might be a good idea for a cat, or a dog house for your dog. Toys will also come in handy during playtime, and will again vary on a pet-by-pet basis.

First time pet-owner?

We've all been there. The demands of having a pet will change based on what kind of pet you have, among other factors. It is important that you do plenty of research. Here are some questions to ask yourself to start off:


  • Are pets allowed at your home?
  • Do you have space for a pet?
  • Do you have time for a pet?
  • Are you financially able to have a pet?

That's just the beginning consisting of macro-details. Some great resources for these and other questions are:

  • Petfinder
  • ASPCA
  • Pet WebMD
